Our Story

From Passion to Profession

What started as a deep fascination with Subaru's legendary boxer engines evolved into Zimbabwe's most trusted Subaru specialist.

At Zaza Rush Subaru, we don't just work on cars—we breathe Subaru. Our journey began with a simple belief: every Subaru owner deserves access to specialist knowledge and genuine parts, not just general mechanics who treat these precision machines like any other vehicle.

Today, from our workshop at Sherwood Crescent in Waterfalls (Zindoga Shops), we serve Subaru enthusiasts across Zimbabwe and beyond, providing everything from routine maintenance to complete engine rebuilds and performance upgrades.

Our Expertise

Why We Specialize in Subaru

Subaru vehicles are engineered differently. They deserve specialists who understand their unique technology inside and out.

Boxer Engine Mastery

Subaru's horizontally-opposed boxer engines are engineering marvels that require specialized knowledge. Our technicians understand the unique maintenance requirements, common issues, and optimal tuning parameters for every EJ and FA series engine.

  • EJ20, EJ25, EJ207, EJ257 specialists
  • FA20 & FA24 direct injection expertise
  • Head gasket & timing belt mastery
  • Performance tuning & builds

AWD System Specialists

Subaru's Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive is the cornerstone of their legendary capability. We understand every variation—from basic automatic AWD to the sophisticated DCCD systems in STI models.

  • Differential service & rebuilds
  • Transfer case maintenance
  • DCCD & VTD system expertise
  • Drivetrain diagnostics

All Generations Covered

Whether you drive a classic GC8 Impreza, a beloved GD/GG Wagon, or the latest VA-series WRX, we have the knowledge and parts to keep your Subaru running perfectly.

  • Classic GC/GF (1992-2000)
  • GD/GG/SG Era (2000-2007)
  • GR/GV/SH Generation (2007-2014)
  • VA/VB Modern Era (2014+)
